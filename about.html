About the Application

GFinds helps you navigate government requirements.

GFinds is a mobile application inspired by eGovPH, specifically designed to help first-time job seekers. This application serves as a complete guide for individuals who need to get their government identifications required by their employers.

GFinds aims to lessen the confusion and burden often experienced during the transition into the professional field.

Disclaimer: Developed exclusively for project purposes. This is an independent academic project and does not hold an official affiliation with government agencies.
